FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats can I upload?
BuddAI supports PDFs, Word documents, PowerPoints, and transcripts of video content. We automatically extract the content and make it available for your chat assistant.
Do I need technical knowledge to set up BuddAI?
No. Setup takes just a few minutes - you upload your documents or videos, and BuddAI trains itself automatically. No code, no technical configuration required.
How does the WhatsApp integration work?
After creating your assistant, you connect it to your number via the WhatsApp Business API. Your customers or employees can then ask questions directly via WhatsApp.
How secure is my data?
Your data is stored exclusively on servers within the European Union. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest (AES-256). We never share your data with third parties or use it to train AI models. BuddAI operates under strict European data protection law - your data never leaves EU infrastructure.
Can I change my plan later?
Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
Do you offer a free trial?
Yes, all plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required.
